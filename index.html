Full Stack Web Developer

I think this is where I'm supposed to write about what makes me unique or whatever. I'm not really unique. I'm just curious and want to work hard and learn and work together with others to solve software problems and help people in some concrete way. I do like run-on sentences sometimes, though.

Skillwise, I am a recent graduate of UT Austin's full stack boot camp and I have been working on my own projects since then (see below for an overview of those skills, see here for my projects). About a year into coding now, I'm still loving every bit of it (OK, not always the bit with CSS). And more than just coding, I love solving problems--the gnarlier the better.

In my free time I read, write, and mess around with Linux. But my two daughters take up most of my free time, so I don't really get a chance to do those things. So, I mostly just code or watch TV if my brain is too fried for it.
